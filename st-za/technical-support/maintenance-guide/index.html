Maintenance Guide

Guidance topic for magnetic separator project review.

Engineering review scope

This guide helps prepare product selection information for African mining, conveyor and industrial sites. It does not replace project-specific confirmation.

Data to confirm

  • Material type and maximum lump size
  • Conveyor belt width, speed and burden depth
  • Suspension height and installation position
  • Dust, humidity, temperature, altitude and corrosion conditions
  • Voltage, frequency and number of phases
